Dessert

Here you will find authentic and delicious Desserts that are called Husik 디저트 , which are usually an assortment of fruit, or fried rice cakes or shaved ice covered with syrup and fruit

부꾸미 Bukkumi (pan-fried rice cake dumplings with sweet red bean paste)

약과 Yakgwa(korean honey pastry)

다식 Dasik ( literally translates to "tea food" was cookies made for royalty in the Silla and Goryeo Dynasty.)
